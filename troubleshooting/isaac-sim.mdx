### GPU driver error — 2026-07-03

**Environment:** Ubuntu 24.04, Isaac Sim 4.x

**Problem:**
Simulation fails to launch with a GPU driver error.

**Cause:**
Driver version mismatch with CUDA requirements.

**Solution:**
Update NVIDIA drivers and restart the machine.

**Links:**
- [NVIDIA driver docs](https://docs.nvidia.com/)
